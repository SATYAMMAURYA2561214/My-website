Our Vision & Mission

The Programming Club is a community of passionate coders who come together to learn, collaborate, and build innovative solutions. Our mission is to foster a culture of continuous learning and problem-solving through coding.

We aim to provide a platform for students to enhance their programming skills, explore new technologies, and work on real-world projects. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced programmer, there's always something new to learn and contribute.

Our club organizes workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and tech talks to help members grow and stay updated with the latest trends in technology.

2020

Programming Club was founded with just 10 members passionate about coding.

2021

Organized our first hackathon with over 100 participants from various colleges.

2022

Expanded to 50+ active members and launched our mentorship program.

2023

Our team won the national-level coding competition and launched our open-source initiative.

2024

Launched our tech blog and conducted over 20 workshops on various programming languages and technologies.
